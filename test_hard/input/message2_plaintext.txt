FROM: Commander, Station X

CLASSIFICATION: TOP SECRET

Intelligence reports confirm enemy fleet movements in the North Atlantic. Convoy HX-229 departed Halifax on 8 March with 40 merchant ships. U-boat wolf packs are believed to be positioned along the expected route.

All stations must maintain 24-hour watch on frequencies 3.5 MHz and 7.2 MHz. Report any intercepts immediately using Protocol Zeta-7.

Remember: loose lips sink ships!
