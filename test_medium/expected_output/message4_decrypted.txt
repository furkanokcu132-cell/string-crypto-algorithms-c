In the middle of the journey of our life, I found myself within a dark forest, for the straight way was lost. Ah, how hard it is to tell what that forest was like, so savage and harsh and dense that the very thought of it renews my fear. It is so bitter that death is hardly more so. But to treat of the good that I found in it, I will tell of the other things I saw there. I cannot rightly tell how I entered it, so full of sleep was I at the point where I abandoned the true way. But when I had reached the foot of a hill where the valley that had so afflicted my heart came to an end, I looked up and saw its shoulders already clothed with the rays of the planet that leads others aright along every road.
